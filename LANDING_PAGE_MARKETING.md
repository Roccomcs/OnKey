# 📣 OnKey Landing - Marketing & Growth Strategy

## 🎯 Pre-Launch Marketing (2-4 semanas antes)

### Content Strategy
```
Week -4:
- Crear blog posts sobre "pain points" de inmobiliarias
- SEO: "Gestión de propiedades", "Alertas de vencimientos", etc.
- Temas:
  * "¿Por qué Excel no es suficiente para gestionar propiedades?"
  * "Cómo evitar perder dinero por contratos vencidos"
  * "5 errores comunes en administración de propiedades"

Week -3:
- Preparar email sequence para early adopters
- Contenido en redes (LinkedIn + Instagram)
- Testimonios grabados (video o texto)

Week -2:
- Soft launch para beta testers
- Recolectar feedback
- Optimizar based on real usage

Week -1:
- Finalize todas las Landing assets
- Anuncio en redes
- Email a contactos
- Press release (si aplica)
```

## 📊 Traffic Sources & Channels

### Owned Channels (Zero Cost)
```
1. Email List
   - Contactos existentes
   - Parte de onboarding
   - Updates periódicas
   - Expected: 2-5% conversion

2. Social Media
   - LinkedIn (profesional, B2B)
   - Instagram (visual, case studies)
   - Twitter/X (faster feedback)
   - Expected: 0.5-1% conversion

3. Direct Outreach
   - Email campaigns
   - Calls (personalizado)
   - Expected: 10-20% conversion (warm)
```

### Paid Channels (Mínimo inversión inicial)
```
1. Google Ads (Search)
   - Keywords: "Software gestión propiedades"
   - "SaaS inmobiliaria"
   - Budget: $500/month para empezar
   - Expected: 2-3% conversion

2. LinkedIn Ads
   - Targeting: Decision makers inmobiliarias
   - Budget: $300/month
   - Expected: 3-5% conversion
   - Lesser effective pero calidad de leads

3. Facebook/Instagram Ads
   - Más conversión, pero lower quality
   - Skip initially, test later
```

### Organic Channels
```
1. SEO (Long-term, takes 3-6 months)
   - Blog posts sobre pain points
   - Backlinks from industry sites
   - Local SEO ("SaaS inmobiliaria [ciudad]")

2. Referrals
   - Early users referring friends
   - Partner network
   - Immobiliaria associations
```

## 📧 Email Marketing Sequences

### Sequence 1: Launch Announcement (Day 1)
```
Subject: "OnKey está aquí. Simplificamos tu gestión."

Body:
- Hook: Problem statement
- Solution: What's OnKey
- Benefit: What they save
- CTA: Link to landing + "Probar gratis"
- Footer: Social proof

Expectation: 15-20% open rate, 3-5% CTR
```

### Sequence 2: Nurture Campaign (If no signup in 7 days)
```
Email 1 (Day 3): "Probaste OnKey?"
- Light reminder
- Tackling an objection
- Link to landing + FAQ

Email 2 (Day 7): "¿Qué te detiene?"
- Addressing top objections
- Shorter, personal tone
- Offer: Meet with founder

Expectation: 10-15% conversion after nurture
```

### Sequence 3: Onboarding (After signup)
```
Email 1 (Immediate): Welcome
- Confirm signup
- Next steps
- Quick start guide link

Email 2 (Day 1): Quick video
- 2-min setup tutorial
- Common questions
- Support contact

Email 3 (Day 5): First feature
- Show a key feature
- How to use it
- Success story

Expectation: 70%+ feature adoption
```

## 💰 Pricing Strategy for Launch

### Dynamic Pricing Approach
```
Phase 1 (Months 1-3): Launch prices
- Starter: Free
- Pro: $39/month (introductory)
  
Phase 2 (After 50+ users): Optimize
- Test: $49, $59, $69
- Analyze MRR impact
- Keep what works

Phase 3 (After 100+ users): Premium tier
- Enterprise: Custom pricing
- Plus features: $99/month
- Annual discounts: 15-20%
```

### Positioning
```
NOT about being cheapest:
- Stripe also isn't the cheapest payment processor
- Notion isn't the cheapest notes app
- Linear isn't the cheapest issue tracker

YOUR positioning: Best value + best experience
```

## 📊 Metrics to Track (First 90 Days)

### Acquisition Metrics
```
- Website traffic (daily, weekly)
- Landing page conversion rate (target: 5-8%)
- Email signups (goal: 30-50/week)
- Trial starts (goal: 100+ on launch day)
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
```

### Product Metrics
```
- Trial completion rate (% who try features)
- Time to first value (avg minutes to first action)
- Feature adoption (% using each key feature)
- Churn rate (% canceling trial)
- NPS (Net Promoter Score)
```

### Business Metrics
```
- MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- Trials converted to paid (goal: 10-20%)
- Lifetime value (LTV)
- Payback period
```

### Quality Metrics
```
- Email quality (% real, engaged users)
- Support tickets (track issues)
- Bug reports (prioritize fixes)
- Feature requests (inform roadmap)
```

## 🎯 90-Day Launch Goals

### Targets (Realistic)
```
Week 1: "Launch week"
- 500+ landing page visitors
- 50+ email signups
- 20+ trial signups
- 0 churned (too early)

Week 2-4: "Build momentum"
- 200-300 weekly visitors
- 15-20 weekly signups
- 5-10 trial conversions to paid
- ~10% trial churn

Month 2: "Growth phase"
- 300-500 weekly visitors
- 30-50 monthly paid users
- ~15% MRR month-over-month growth
- Identify top 3 traffic sources

Month 3: "Optimization phase"
- 400-700 weekly visitors
- 50+ paid users
- 25-30% MRR growth
- Clear best performing channels
```

### Success Indicators
- Email list growing (100+/week)
- Paid users sustained
- Engagement remains high (not just signups)
- Receiving testimonials naturalemente
- Feature requests coming in

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

```
❌ Launching without tracking setup
❌ Changing everything after week 1 (not enough data)
❌ Not responding to feedback quick
❌ Spending heavily on ads before organic channels
❌ Relying only on social media for traffic
❌ Being invisible for months (hard to get attention backtrack)
❌ Not following up with signups who don't trial
❌ Ignoring negative feedback / objections
❌ Over-promising in copy
❌ Not asking users "Why did you cancel?"
```
